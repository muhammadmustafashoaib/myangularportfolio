Register Your National Investor Number (NIN)

Follow the steps below to easily apply.

Step 1: Apply for Your NIN

Use your existing information to start the application. It's quick and easy.

Step 2: Provide Extra Info

If required by your chosen exchange, submit additional details to complete your registration.

Step 3: Submit Your Application

Connect to the exchange platforms and submit your application to finalize your NIN creation.
